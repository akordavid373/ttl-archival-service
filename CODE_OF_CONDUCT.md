# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our Standards

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all
contributors. Please be respectful and considerate of others.

### Expected Behavior

- **Be welcoming**: Help newcomers learn and contribute
- **Be respectful**: Treat others with dignity and respect
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- **Be constructive**: Provide helpful feedback and suggestions
- **Be collaborative**: Work together to solve problems
- **Be professional**: Maintain professional communication

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- **Discrimination**: Discriminatory language or actions
- **Personal attacks**: Insults, derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- **Spam**: Unrelated content, self-promotion, or off-topic discussions
- **Privacy violations**: Sharing private information without consent
- **Threats**: Any form of threatening language or behavior

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community forums and chat channels
- Meetups and events (online or in-person)
- Any other community-organized activities

## Enforcement

### Reporting Guidelines

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please:

1. **Report privately**: Contact the maintainers directly
2. **Be specific**: Provide details about the incident
3. **Be timely**: Report as soon as possible after the incident

### Enforcement Process

1. **Review**: Maintainers will review all reports
2. **Investigation**: We will investigate the situation thoroughly
3. **Action**: We will take appropriate action based on the findings
4. **Follow-up**: We will follow up with all parties involved

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- **Warning**: Formal warning about behavior
- **Temporary suspension**: Temporary removal from community spaces
- **Permanent ban**: Permanent removal from community spaces
- **Report to authorities**: For serious violations
